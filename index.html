Some Speak




Education is not the preparation for life, education is life itself." - John Dewey



Having strong conviction and belief in these words of Dewey, today I find myself grown globally with my students whose ongoing explorations in the educational arena has found a new dimension to take this school to newer and greater heights. Today's education is planned for the children who can win the world through adjusting and accommodating themselves in a global curriculum being imparted in classrooms. A child who is educated in this modern age is expected to learn the practical and application based studies rather than depend on the formal orthodox curriculum. The progressive and the new age education that we in Techno India Group public School, Hooghly follow is facilitating our students with an opening to face new challenges and opportunities to fight and overcome the hardships and crises of this ruthless pragmatic world. These opportunities the students get to do and learn through the different activities throughout the year which we technically call Life Skill Activities. Today the CBSE curriculum has been designed and framed in such a manner that the students and teachers are learning to comprehend education by attaining knowledge for excellence and education for quality in global terms. This school prepares students to be culturally enriched, to develop qualities of self-esteem, determination to achieve and overall imbibing the motto Stay Local but Think and Act Global. The parents support in taking this school ahead is also praiseworthy. I take immense pride in mentioning that this school is the most established, reputed and acclaimed school in this district.
All The Best to All!
-MS. PRADEEPTA CHATTERJEE
